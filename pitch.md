# SUMTEO (Solana) - Pitch Notes

## One-liner
SUMTEO turns reading into a visible, social growth experience using onchain reflection NFTs.

## Problem
- Most people start books but do not finish.
- Reading is lonely and easy to drop.
- Reading history is fragmented across apps.

## Solution
- Reflection NFT: favorite line + one sentence.
- Tree DNA: reflections shape a unique tree per book.
- Team forest: weekly goals grow a shared tree.

## Why Solana
- Low cost, high-frequency minting
- Fast confirmations for consumer UX
- NFT ecosystem fits personal identity

## Market
- TAM: $80B+ personal growth market
- SAM: 200M+ digital readers
- SOM: Solana consumer users

## Hypotheses
- H1: Social visibility increases reading consistency.
- H2: Team reading improves retention.
- H3: Onchain permanence increases commitment.

## Validation Plan
- 2-3 pilot reading groups
- Track weekly completion and 4-week retention
- Measure reflection rate and team engagement

## Roadmap
MVP -> Pilot -> Onchain mint -> Team forest -> Social sharing
